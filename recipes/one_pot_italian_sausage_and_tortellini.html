One-Pot Italian Sausage and Tortellini

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 (19 oz) package cheese tortellini
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the Italian sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until softened and fragrant.
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes and tomato sauce. Cook for another 2 minutes, letting the flavors combine.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and bring the mixture to a gentle boil.
  5. Add the cheese tortellini to the pot. Cook according to package directions, stirring occasionally, until the tortellini are tender.
  6. Lower the heat and stir in the heavy cream and baby spinach. Cook until the spinach is wilted.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over the top, cover, and let it melt.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
